I am an Assistant Professor at Department of Computer Science, San Diego State University.

My research interest lies in the intersection of computational linguistics, psycholinguistics, and cognitive sciences. I use computational techniques, such as machine learning, to study language use, language change and adaptation under different time scales.

  • Language styles in dialogue reflects the cognitive mechanisms of our brain to comprehend and product language, which interact closely with a range of non-language factors: social power, acquaintance etc.
